How to Protect Yourself

  • Never click on suspicious links from unknown senders
  • Don't share personal or financial information through WhatsApp
  • Enable two-factor authentication on your WhatsApp account
  • Report spam messages directly within WhatsApp
  • Educate your contacts about these scams
